Step to step for a great lasagna recipe

The recipe consists of adding cheese and tomato sauce with a little bit of meat. Then you put in the oven at 180 degrees for an hour to get a crispy and crunchy lasagna

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Meat
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Salt

Description

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees.
  2. In a baking dish, layer the ingredients starting with a layer of sauce, then noodles, cheese, and meat. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used.
  3. Finish with a layer of cheese on top.
  4. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 30 minutes.
  5. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 30 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.
  6. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
