Step to step for a great pizza recipe

The recipe consists of getting the dough ready, adding some great quality cheese and pepperoni on top, including tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Tomato sauce
  • Wheat
  • Flour

Description

  1. Make a tasty dough with wheat, flour, and some olive oil
  2. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough
  3. Add a generous layer of cheese on top of the sauce
  4. Place the pepperoni slices evenly over the cheese
  5. Bake in a preheated oven at 220 degrees Celsius for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned
  6. Add pepperoni on top, let it cool for a few minutes and enjoy!
